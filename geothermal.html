What is Geothermal Energy?

Geothermal energy uses the heat and pressure generated from the earths hotter, deeper layers. Nearly all of this type of energy comes from the mantle, the first layer under the crust of the earth. This mantle consists of extremely dense and hot materials, primarily magma. This heat and pressure can build up and vent out things like geysers, hot springs, and steam vents. And in turn we can use that heat and steam to power generators or charge batteries.
